Warzone Tournament Rules

Updated: Wednesday 29/10/2020

Join your captain's lobby and be ready to drop into Verdansk after 20:00

This week's playlist: BR Trio's

Format

  • Teams play 5 games - all 5 games count towards your final score
  • Tournaments will take place every Thursday
  • Entry fee - Free
  • Tournament starts at 20:00 - please drop shortly after

Points

  • Points are earned through final placement and team kills
  • 1 Point per Placement from 30th position – 1st position. Example: 25th Place Finish returns 6 points
  • Top 5 Placement Bonus awarded:
    • 1st = +15
    • 2nd = +12
    • 3rd = +9
    • 4th = +6
    • 5th = +3
  • Points per kill (Team cumulative)
    • 1-5 Kills = 1pt per kill
    • 6-10 Kills = 2pts per kill
    • 11-15 Kills = 3pts per kill
    • 16+ Kills = 4pts per kill
  • In the event scores are tied. Winner will be decided by total team kills. If total kills are tied, winner will be determined by lowest accumulative placement over the 5 games.
  • Team and Individual scores can be tracked via the live table and team scoresheets. Please also record your stats by taking a photo as a backup and share via captain’s chat on WhatsApp. Please send the link to all your team members so they can view the live scores and standings in the tournament. This will show individual kills, overall kills, placement position and overall points per game.

Tracking matches

  • The match you play needs to finish before the data is available to the app, so it won't show straight away depending your placement.
  • If there's a discrepency, first check your match directly on our source: http://cod.tracker.gg
  • In the very unlikely event that your match isn't recorded on the official Call of Duty app or Tracker.gg (above) then you will have to play an extra game. Unfortunately if the game servers do not record your match there's not much we can do about this.

Rules

  • Each team needs a team captain. This captain will have their matches tracked during the tournament
  • Captain's must host their squad
  • Captains need to send a picture of their final scoreboard each game to the WhatsApp group. This picture MUST include the position your team placed and every players kills. This will be 2 pictures as the placement is shown before the scoreboard.
  • Cross-play must be enabled.
  • You cannot restart your game if you come across a hacker. Not every team has a way of capturing footage to prove they have been killed by a hacker so this cannot be monitored properly..
  • A team may restart the match if they are experiencing poor connection issues when they are in the warm-up lobby, you MUST notify the Captains chat if you restart. As soon as your squad is in the plane ready to drop you cannot leave and this will count as one of your 5 games/scores.
  • A team may also restart the match if a player loses connection during the game. If you do so, please can the player who got disconnected send a picture of any error message they got showing the disconnection. Or if one of the players still in the game can quickly take a picture of the left-hand side where it pops up saying for example ‘AdamSmith123 has timed out’. ​
  • You may join a tournament with less players, but you must still play on the same playlist as everyone else with it set to ‘don’t fill’.
  • If any team is found to be cheating, you will be disqualified and neither yourself nor anyone in your team will be allowed to enter the tournament again.
